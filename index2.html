Pricing

The designer calculates the overall price of the project inclusive of products and services. Based on the total cost, we will charge around 6%-15% as a design fee. This can shoot upto 20% based on the level of design and expertise involved.

Exterior

Decoration

All clients experience complete and personalized interior design help beginning with an initial one-on-one consultation and interactive questionnaire to start. You receive design concepts from multiple designers based on your requirements and budget with constant designer communication.

Planning

Space planning is a fundamental element of the interior design process. It starts with an in-depth analysis of how the space is to be used. The designer then draws up a plan that defines the zones of the space and the activities that will take place in those zones.

Execution

Our execution process promotes responsible design, sustainable materials and healthy environments.
